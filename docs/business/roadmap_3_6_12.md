# Roadmap (3 / 6 / 12 Months)

## 3 Months: Pilot-Ready Wallet Layer

Goal: convert the hackathon prototype into a real pilot candidate.

- Run one pilot with a wallet, super app, or payout partner
- Finalize score calibration and route thresholds with partner fraud policy
- Add English plus a Bahasa Melayu template pack for Malaysia-based pilots
- Produce weekly latency and false-positive monitoring
- Add operator logging for `Approve`, `Flag`, and `Block` outcomes

## 6 Months: Segment-Specific Expansion

Goal: improve accuracy and operational fit by user segment.

- Separate policies for gig workers, QR merchants, and new wallet users
- Add richer contextual signals such as device reputation and velocity rules
- Add reviewer console for fraud ops teams
- Support country-specific message templates and compliance notes
- Prepare expansion playbooks for Thailand, Malaysia, or Indonesia depending on partner access

## 12 Months: Regional Productization

Goal: turn PocketSignal into a multi-tenant ASEAN fraud trust layer.

- Multi-tenant deployment for multiple wallet partners
- Shared fraud graph and consortium-style intelligence, with privacy controls
- Country-specific threshold packs and model calibration
- Edge or partner-VPC deployment for low-connectivity environments
- Full audit trail for explainability, privacy, and regulatory review

## What This Roadmap Shows Judges

PocketSignal is not a one-off demo.

It has a credible path from:
- student prototype
- to pilot product
- to region-ready infrastructure for wallet trust and fraud recovery
