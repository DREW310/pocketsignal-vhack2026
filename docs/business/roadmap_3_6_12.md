# Roadmap (3 / 6 / 12 Months)

## 3 Months: Malaysia Pilot-Ready Layer

Goal: turn the hackathon prototype into a real Malaysia pilot candidate.

- Pilot one wallet, super app, QR acquirer, or payout workflow
- Finalize calibrated route mix with partner fraud-policy constraints
- Ship English plus Bahasa Melayu recovery templates
- Add weekly route-mix and false-positive monitoring
- Add operator logging for `Approve`, `Flag`, `Block`, and user-confirmation outcomes

## 6 Months: Segment-Specific Expansion

Goal: improve accuracy and operational fit by user segment.

- Separate policies for gig workers, QR merchants, and new wallet users
- Benchmark lighter local models for richer wording latency
- Add reviewer tooling for denied or escalated gray-zone cases
- Add country-specific message templates and compliance notes
- Prepare expansion playbooks for Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines

## 12 Months: Regional Productization

Goal: turn PocketSignal into a multi-tenant ASEAN fraud-trust layer.

- Multi-tenant deployment for multiple wallet partners
- Shared fraud graph and consortium-style intelligence with privacy controls
- Country-specific threshold packs and calibration policies
- Partner-VPC or edge-style deployment options for sensitive environments
- Full audit trail for explainability, privacy, and regulatory review

## What This Roadmap Shows Judges

PocketSignal is not just a student demo.

It has a credible path from:
- Malaysia pilot
- to segment-aware operational product
- to regional infrastructure for wallet trust and fraud recovery
